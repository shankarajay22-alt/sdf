1 · Photo

Capture tips for best accuracy
  • Top-down view, lens parallel to the beads (no tilt).
  • Plain white, matte background; even, soft lighting.
  • Avoid hard reflections/glare on the glass.
  • Keep a ruler or known-size reference in frame.
  • Get close — each bead should be large and sharp.

2 · Calibration required

Tap two points on the image that are a known distance apart (e.g. two ruler marks). Then enter that distance below.
